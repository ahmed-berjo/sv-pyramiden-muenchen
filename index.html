Our Story

More Than a Football Club

SV Pyramiden München is an amateur football club in Munich, founded by Egyptians with the aim of connecting cultures through sport and fostering a strong community spirit.

By combining a passion for football with a deep sense of heritage, the club promotes diversity, mutual respect, and integration within the wider Munich community.
